Atlas Rewards App — Official Build Roadmap

White-label client retention & rewards SaaS · cookie-cutter clonable across industries

Platform: PWA (now) → Atlas Engine native shell (later) Backend: Supabase Tenancy: Single app, sub-account routing Checkpoints: 12
Phase A
Foundation
1 checkpoint · everything else depends on this
CHECKPOINT 1
Supabase schema + multi-tenant routing
Database, auth, role separation, and the routing that makes every URL resolve to the right business.
  • Tables: businesses, users, memberships, members, points_ledger, rewards, redemptions, referrals, events
  • Roles: agency_admin, business_manager, customer
  • Row-Level Security policies per role
  • Subdomain or path routing → resolves business_id
  • Auth flows: magic link, password, social
Blocks: every other checkpoint
Gate A → B: You sign off on the schema and see a working multi-tenant routing demo before Phase B starts.
Phase B
Brand layer
2 checkpoints · makes the app feel "owned" by each business
CHECKPOINT 2
Brand customization engine
Logo, colors, business name, welcome message, industry preset, services catalog.
  • Brand config stored on businesses row
  • Industry presets: medspa, gym, restaurant, arcade, salon, dental, home service
  • Theme system reads brand at runtime
  • Service / product catalog per business
Depends on: CP 1
CHECKPOINT 3
Customer dashboard (Client view)
Mobile-first PWA dashboard with the big points card, tier, progress, and CTAs.
  • Main points card: name, logo, balance, tier, progress bar
  • "Earn More Points" and "Redeem Reward" CTAs
  • Member signup & login (magic link)
  • PWA install prompt + offline shell
Depends on: CP 1, CP 2
Gate B → C: You see a fully branded customer dashboard for at least one test business before the points engine is built.
Phase C
Earn & redeem engine
5 checkpoints · CP 5–8 can run in parallel once CP 4 ships
CHECKPOINT 4 · CORE
Points rules engine
Configurable rules per business, atomic ledger, idempotent transactions.
  • Rule types: review, referral, birthday, visit, purchase, social, profile
  • Per-business rule definitions (JSON config)
  • Atomic points_ledger with idempotency keys
  • Tier engine: thresholds, perks, progression
Depends on: CP 1 · Blocks: CP 5, 6, 7, 8, 11
CHECKPOINT 5
Rewards store + redemption
Per-business rewards catalog, redemption with verification.
  • Reward types: discount, free service/item, VIP perk, upgrade, custom
  • Redemption flow: deduct points → issue code/QR
  • Manager-side verify & mark fulfilled
  • Audit trail in ledger
Depends on: CP 4
CHECKPOINT 6
Referral tracking
Unique link per member, attribution, anti-fraud.
  • Unique referral code/link per member
  • Attribution at signup or first qualifying action
  • Guards: self-referral, duplicate device, velocity
Depends on: CP 4
CHECKPOINT 7
Review reward flow
Post-visit prompt → Google review → verify → award.
  • Per-business Google review link
  • Verification: screenshot upload OR link confirm
  • Reward awarded once verified, idempotent
Depends on: CP 4
CHECKPOINT 8
Milestone + reactivation
Birthday, anniversary, Nth visit, 30/60/90 day dormancy.
  • Scheduled jobs (Supabase cron / edge functions)
  • Triggers: birthday, anniversary, first/5th/10th visit
  • Dormancy bands: 30, 60, 90 days inactive
  • Fires comeback offer through CP 12 channels
Depends on: CP 4
Gate C → D: A customer can earn points multiple ways, see them on the dashboard, and redeem a reward end-to-end before the agency UI is built.
Phase D
Agency power tools
2 checkpoints · cookie-cutter builder + analytics
CHECKPOINT 9
Widget toggle builder (Agency view)
Per-business panel to enable/disable any client-app widget. Live preview.
  • Toggle: points card, rewards store, referrals, reviews, birthdays, visit tracker, booking CTA, offers, leaderboard, push, SMS
  • Live preview pane mirrors the actual client app
  • One-click clone from a template business
Depends on: CP 2, CP 5
CHECKPOINT 10
Analytics dashboard
Agency rollups across all sub-accounts; Manager view for their business only.
  • Repeat visits, referrals, reviews, points issued/redeemed
  • Revenue attributed to offers
  • Dormant customers recovered
  • Customer lifetime value estimate
Depends on: CP 5, 6, 7
Gate D → E: Agency can spin up a brand-new sub-account, configure it visually, and see live analytics before integrations are wired.
Phase E
Integrations & launch
2 checkpoints · GHL, webhooks, messaging, white-label deploy
CHECKPOINT 11
GoHighLevel + webhooks
Two-way GHL contact sync, inbound & outbound event webhooks.
  • GHL contact sync per sub-account
  • Inbound webhooks: purchases, bookings, reviews, referrals
  • Outbound webhooks: points awarded, redeemed, tier-up
  • Per-business webhook secrets & replay protection
Depends on: CP 4
CHECKPOINT 12
Push / SMS / email + launch polish
Web push, Twilio (or GHL relay), email, automation rules, then white-label launch.
  • PWA web push (VAPID)
  • SMS via Twilio or GHL relay
  • Email provider integration
  • Automation rule engine: "when X happens, send Y"
  • White-label deployment process & agency onboarding docs
Depends on: CP 8, CP 11
Future · Atlas Engine native shell: Once the PWA is live, build a thin iOS + Android container app called Atlas Engine. Customer opens it → QR-scans or looks up a business → the branded PWA loads inside and becomes their permanent view. App-store distribution without rebuilding the rewards logic.

Three views, one app

Agency view
Cookie-cutter builder. Spin up sub-accounts, customize brand, toggle widgets, view rollup analytics, manage all clients.
Manager view
Per-business owner. Sees their own customers, redeems rewards in person, views their analytics, manages services.
Client view
The branded PWA each customer uses. Points card, rewards store, referrals, reviews — all themed to the business.
